## 💡 Pro Tips for Collaboration

1. **Over-communicate**: Better to share too much than too little
2. **Assume good intent**: We're all working toward the same goals
3. **Be specific**: "Can you review invoice #123 by 2 PM?" not "Can you look at some invoices?"
4. **Follow up**: If you don't hear back, ask again politely
5. **Say thank you**: Acknowledge each other's contributions
